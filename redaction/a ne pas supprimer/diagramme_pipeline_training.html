Hyperparamètres Optimisés

  • Epochs: 30 (early stop at 11)
  • Batch size: 32
  • Learning rate: 0.0001 (Adam optimizer)
  • Adversarial epsilon: 0.08 (training) / 0.1 (test)
  • Mix ratio: 70% clean, 30% FGSM, 0% PGD
  • Dropout: 0.45
  • Gradient clipping: max_norm=1.0
  • Differential Privacy: Disabled (performance impact)
